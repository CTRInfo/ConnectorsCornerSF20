• Electronic Payroll Distribution Reallocation (e-PDR) is live
• Allows all departments to view allocations that are hitting their departments, SPF's or project ID
• Allows the reviewer to approve or deny the allocation
• Please contact the GFA that initiated the form if you have questions about the SPF, project ID or department charged before approving your particular line(s)
• Only one ePDR for an individual can be created and routed for approvals at a time
• View the e-PDR Approvals Job Aid

• PI effort certification for FY21 Quarter 1 begins Sunday, November 8th and ends Sunday, November 22nd
• Please contact your GFA if you have questions regarding your allocations or those of your staff that you certify
• Please review and certify as soon as possible once effort certification opens

• Now live as of October 1, 2020
• Plan codes replaced with Health Plans
• Instead of all numeric, the Health Plans will be a combination of alpha-numeric.
• Studies Executed on or prior to 10/1: send all Health Plan Request Forms to CTO@childrensnational.org. Contact your GFA for a Health Plan Request form.
• New Studies: Clinical Trials Office will send Health Plan Request Form at the time of protocol build triggered by IRB/Study Team.
• Once PID/SPF is confirmed, CTO will request Business Operations for Health Plan.
• CTO will provide study team with customized billing grid and Health Plan.
• Contact CTO@childrensnational.org with any questions. 

Are you ready to submit a proposal?

• Connect with your Grants and Finance Administrator (GFA). Unsure who your assigned Grants and Contracts Specialist is? Contact your Business Manager, Christina Nobleman or view the list of principal investigators assigned to the GFA under the Business Office section.

• Ask your GFA to make sure that you have a BearGrants account by logging in. Once your proposal is prepared by your GFA, you will need to log in to BearGrants to review and certify your proposal.
• For NIH and AHRQ proposals, create an eRA Commons account by asking your GFA to request one from the Grants & Contracts Specialist (GCS.)
